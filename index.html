Welcome to BroodGuard

BroodGuard is a youth-led poultry startup dedicated to improving poultry farming in Nigeria through sustainable, smart, and affordable solutions.

What We Do

  • 🌾 Innovate climate-smart poultry practices
  • 🐣 Improve feeding systems and health monitoring
  • 🎓 Educate farmers and empower youth
  • 🌍 Tackle food insecurity through clean agriculture

Why We Exist

Founded by Wisdom Lebechi, BroodGuard was born out of a passion for farming, biology, and helping communities thrive. With over 300 birds sold and ₦3 million in earnings, we’re not just growing chickens — we’re growing opportunity, knowledge, and change.

Explore our work, read our blog, and discover how youth can lead the future of agriculture — one chick at a time.
